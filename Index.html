What is Spotify?

Spotify is an app where you can listen to songs or podcasts. Spotify released in 2008 and as of 2023, Spotify has gained over 489 monthly active users which makes it the number 1 music app. Songs that get published gets paid by a royalty as much as $0.003 - $0.005 per stream.

What is the most streamed song on Spotify?

Spotify has been out for almost 15 years, it's no wonder there's so many songs there. Songs have gained millions and even billions of streams, from that, has that ever made you wonder what's the most streamed song on there?

1. Blinding Lights - 3.506 Billion Streams

Blinding Lights is a song performed by The Weeknd. Ever since it's release on November 29, 2019, the song has gained over 3 Billion Streams on Spotify and has gained the title as the most streamed spotify song since the end of 2022

2. Shape of You - 3.433 Billion Streams

Shape of you is a song by Ed Sheeran on the 6th of January, 2017. The song won 5 awards, 1 of them was for the most streamed song on the 22nd of December, 2021. Ever since being passed by Blinding Lights, it's very unlikely it will lose it's title as the 2nd most streamed song on Spotify.

3. Dance Monkey - 2.790 Billion Streams

Dance Monkey is a song by Tones and I. This song was posted on the 10th of May, 2019. Ever since the release, this song has gained over 2.790 Billion and is on track to pass 3 billion views.

4. Someone You Loved - 2.723 Billion Streams

Someone You Loved is a song performed by Lewis Capaldi and published on the November 8, 2018. Since it's release, the song has gained 2.7B Billion streams and is on track to surpass Dance Monkey.

5. Rockstar - 2.642 Billion Streams

Rockstar is a song by Post Malone featuring 21 Savage made on September 15, 2017. Since it's release, the song has gained over 2.6 Billion streams. As of now, the song remains as the 5th most streamed song on spotify.
